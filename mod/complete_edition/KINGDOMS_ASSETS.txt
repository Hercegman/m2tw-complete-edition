================================================================
  STEP 6: PORTING FACTIONS FROM KINGDOMS
  (Wales, Ireland, Jerusalem, Norway/Sweden)
================================================================

Advantage: these factions ALREADY EXIST in the Kingdoms campaigns with
finished banners, UI, units and names. You are not building anything
from scratch - you just copy the assets and register the faction.

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHERE THEY LIVE (Steam, main game folder -> mods\):
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Wales, Ireland   ->  mods\britannia\
  Jerusalem        ->  mods\crusades\
  Norway, Denmark  ->  mods\teutonic\  (+ Denmark already in vanilla)

----------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT TO PORT FOR EACH FACTION:
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. BANNERS AND UI:
   from  mods\<campaign>\data\ui\<faction>\
   to    mods\croatia_overhaul\data\ui\<faction>\
   (the whole faction folder - contains banners, symbols, cards)

2. SYMBOLS:
   data\ui\symbols\symbol_<faction>.tga
   data\ui\rebel_symbols\
   data\ui\loading_screen\symbols\

3. FACTION DEFINITION:
   from <campaign>\data\descr_sm_factions.txt copy the whole
   block of that faction (e.g. "faction wales ... ") and paste it
   into our descr_sm_factions.txt.

4. UNITS (optional but recommended):
   - export_descr_unit.txt  (unit stats)
   - export_descr_buildings.txt (who can build what)
   - data\units\ (unit models/textures for that faction)
   - data\unit_models\
   Port only the entries related to that faction.

5. NAMES:
   from <campaign>\data\text\ pull the faction, general and
   settlement names into our expanded.txt / names.txt.

----------------------------------------------------------------
KEY THINGS TO WATCH:
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Kingdoms cultures: Wales/Ireland = northern_european.
  Jerusalem = has its own logic (crusader states).
  Make sure the culture exists in our mod.
- Faction names must be UNIQUE. If both vanilla and Kingdoms
  use the same internal name, use the one that already
  exists - do not duplicate.
- After adding, you MUST register them in descr_strat.txt
  (playable) + assign a region in descr_regions.txt.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SLOT MATH (keep an eye on it):
----------------------------------------------------------------

  Norway (+ Sweden if you want):  +1-2 = 28-29
